      Guide & Methodology

      Platform Overview

      The Global Innovation Intelligence Radar is an autonomous AI horizon-scanning tool. It continuously crawls the web to discover, extract, and evaluate global innovations—ranging from unregistered grassroots inventions to institutional solutions.

      AI Engine & Execution

      Powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash with real-time Google Search Grounding.

      • Executes autonomously on a scheduled multi-day tracker.
      • Queries over 60 specific keywords across 10 thematic categories.
      • Normalizes data, prevents duplicates via MD5 hashing, extracts geospatial coordinates.

      Metrics & Scoring

      Priority Score (0-100)
      Composite index: Impact (40%) + Risk (40%) + Replicability (20%).
      Risk Assessment
      Evaluates hazards (toxic, fire, chemical, environmental) associated with each innovation.
      Hidden Gems
      Grassroots innovations with high impact and low replicability cost.

      Intelligence Resume Tab

      The Intelligence Resume tab synthesizes all collected data into a periodic analytical report. It is generated every 90 days (configurable) and provides: innovation mix breakdown, top categories, risk analysis, geographic insights, hidden gems, intervention opportunities, and strategic recommendations.
