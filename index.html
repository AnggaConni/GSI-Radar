      Guide & Methodology

      Platform Overview

      The Global Innovation Intelligence Radar is an autonomous AI horizon-scanning tool. It continuously crawls the web to discover, extract, and evaluate global innovations—ranging from unregistered grassroots inventions to institutional solutions.

      AI Engine & Execution

      Powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash with real-time Google Search Grounding.

      • Executes autonomously on a scheduled multi-day tracker.
      • Queries over 60 specific keywords across 10 thematic categories (e.g., Agriculture, Health, Environment).
      • Normalizes data, prevents duplicate entries via MD5 hashing, and extracts precise geospatial coordinates.

      Metrics & Scoring

      Every innovation discovered is rigorously analyzed by the AI pipeline across four main axes. These metrics fuel the Smart Visualization on every nodecard and detail modal.

      Priority Score (0-100)
      A composite index combining Impact (40%), Risk Assessment (40%), and Replicability (20%). Higher scores indicate urgent priority for observation or intervention.
      Risk Assessment
      Evaluates potential hazards (toxic, environmental, fire, etc.) associated with the innovation. Highly critical for unvetted grassroots DIY projects.
      Hidden Gems
      A special classification automatically awarded to grassroots innovations that feature high impact alongside low cost replicability.
