      Methodology & Framework

      Platform Overview

      The Global Innovation Intelligence Radar is an autonomous OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) scanning tool. Scheduled via GitHub Actions, it utilizes Google Gemini AI with real-time Google Search Grounding to discover, extract, and evaluate global unrecorded innovations—ranging from rural grassroots inventions to institutional pilot solutions.

      The 5-Layer AI Discovery Pipeline

      Pass 1 Validation & Authentication

      Filters raw web search results. The AI strictly determines if the content represents a real-world, tangible innovation that solves a clear problem (requires >60% confidence).

      Pass 2 Deep Data Extraction

      Extracts structural parameters: step-by-step processes, material lists, geographical impact targets, replicability scale, and secures original, direct source URLs.

      Pass 3 Conservative Risk Assessment

      Evaluates potential hazards including fire, toxic exposure, environmental leaching, and structural failure. Automatically flags initiatives needing safety intervention.

      Pass 4 Knowledge Lineage Tracing

      Determines the origin of the foundational knowledge: Is it Traditional Knowledge, Self-Taught ingenuity, Adapted from existing norms, or derived from Formal Education?

      Pass 5 Algorithmic Synthesis

      Calculates the composite Priority Score, assigns Critical Safety Flags, tags Hidden Gems, and normalizes geospatial coordinates (lat/lon) via Nominatim API.

      Scoring & Categorization Matrices

      Priority Score (0-100)

      A composite strategic index calculated from:
      Impact Scale (40%) + Risk Penalty (40%) + Ease of Replicability (20%). Determines the item's ranking.

      Hidden Gems

      Automatically awarded to innovations that are exclusively Grassroots, possess a Low Cost Level, and score a minimum of 6/10 on the Impact scale.

      Critical Danger Flag

      Triggered if an innovation scores ≥ 8 on the Risk scale AND involves hazardous keywords (fire, chemical, explosion, toxic).

      Periodic Intelligence Resume

      An AI Analyst script reads the entire database periodically (default: 90 days) to synthesize trends, highlight emerging risks, and generate actionable intervention recommendations.

      Thematic Scan Domains

      The crawler cycles randomly through over 60 specific search-grounding keywords spanning 10 global impact sectors:
